Cyberpunk 2077: The Complete Story

Introduction: Choosing Your Path

At the start, players choose from three lifepaths:

  • Nomad: Wanderers from the desert areas outside Night City.
  • Streetkid: Hustlers who grew up on the streets of Night City.
  • Corpo: Ruthless employees from the corporate world of Arasaka.

Meet V: The Protagonist

V is a mercenary aiming for fame and fortune in Night City. During the story, they acquire a unique implant called the Relic, which contains the digital consciousness of Johnny Silverhand, a rebellious rockstar played by Keanu Reeves.

Main Story

The Big Heist

V teams up with Jackie Welles to steal the Relic from Arasaka Corporation. Things go south, leading to Jackie’s death and V being forced to use the Relic to survive.

Johnny's Arrival

The implant introduces Johnny Silverhand’s consciousness, creating a tense relationship as they navigate a race against time to save V’s life.

Key Characters

  • Judy Alvarez: A skilled techie and ally.
  • Panam Palmer: A nomad with a rebellious streak.
  • Rogue Amendiares: A legendary mercenary from Johnny’s past.
  • Goro Takemura: A former Arasaka bodyguard seeking redemption.

Endings

The story offers multiple endings based on your choices:

  • Revenge: Team up with Johnny to destroy Arasaka.
  • Corporate Alliance: Work with Arasaka to save your life.
  • Freedom: Abandon everything and live on your terms.
  • Tragic Sacrifice: Sacrifice yourself to save others.
